About Our School

Founded in 1985, Greenwood High School has been a cornerstone of educational excellence in our community. Our mission is to foster intellectual growth, creativity, and character development in a supportive and challenging environment.

With a student-teacher ratio of 12:1, we ensure personalized attention for every student. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for the challenges of higher education and beyond.
